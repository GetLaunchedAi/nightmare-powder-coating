About Us

Experience the Nightmare Powder Coating Difference

At Nightmare Powder Coating, we’re driven by a passion for delivering the most durable and visually striking finishes in the industry. With over 26 years of collective experience in powder coating, Cerakote application, and abrasive blasting, we’ve built a reputation for precision, quality, and exceptional customer service. Based in Carson City, MI, we proudly serve a diverse range of clients, from individual enthusiasts to large-scale industrial partners.

Our journey began with a commitment to mastering the art and science of advanced surface finishes. We understand that a superior coating isn't just about aesthetics; it's about providing unmatched protection, extending component lifespan, and enhancing performance. That’s why we continuously invest in state-of-the-art equipment and training, ensuring every project, no matter the size, meets our rigorous standards.

"Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction is at the core of everything we do. We believe in providing solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations, ensuring every piece leaves our facility with the 'Nightmare' seal of unparalleled durability."
