Professional Abrasive Blasting Services

The Critical First Step for Every Project

Before any coating can be applied, the surface must be meticulously cleaned and prepared. Our in-house sandblasting services use various media to effectively remove old finishes, rust, scale, and other contaminants.

This process creates a uniform, clean, and textured surface that is essential for a strong mechanical bond between the part and the new coating. Without this vital step, even the highest quality coatings can fail prematurely.



Our Sandblasting Process

  1. Expert evaluation of the material and existing finish
  2. Selection of the appropriate abrasive media
  3. Thorough blasting to remove all contaminants
  4. Final air-blast and wipe down for a clean surface

Benefits of Professional Sandblasting

  • Creates an ideal profile for coating adhesion
  • Removes rust, corrosion, and old paint
  • Ensures a long-lasting and durable final finish
  • Prepares parts for a flawless application
