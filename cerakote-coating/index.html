Cerakote is a specialized ceramic-based finish that can be applied to metals, plastics, polymers, and wood. It provides a highly durable, corrosion-resistant, and aesthetically pleasing finish with superior performance.

Got questions about our Cerakote services? Here are some of the most common questions we receive.

  • Cerakote is a ceramic-based, thin-film coating applied as a liquid and thermally cured. Powder coating is a dry powder that is electrostatically applied and baked on. Cerakote is thinner and offers higher performance in certain applications, such as high-temperature parts.
